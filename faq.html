Common Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your move with St. George's most trusted team.

Booking & Scheduling

How do I book a move?

You can book by calling us directly at (435) 862-0699 or by filling out the "Get Quote" form on our website. We recommend calling for the fastest response.

How much notice do you need?

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for weekends or end-of-month dates. However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests when our schedule permits.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. Please notify us at least 48 hours in advance to cancel or reschedule without a penalty. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a deposit forfeiture.

Pricing & Payment

How are your rates structured?

We offer hourly rates for local moves ($105 - $150/hr depending on crew size) and flat rates for long-distance moves. The clock starts when we arrive at your door and stops when the job is done.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and Venmo. Payment is due upon completion of the move.

Is a deposit required?

Yes, a small deposit is required to secure your date on our calendar. This deposit is credited toward your final bill.

Services & Equipment

What equipment do you provide?

We come fully equipped with hand trucks, furniture dollies, thick moving blankets, shrink wrap, tape, and tools for assembly/disassembly. This is included in our service.

Do you move specialty items like pianos or safes?

Yes! We are trained and equipped to move upright pianos, gun safes (up to 600 lbs), and antiques. Please mention these items when booking so we bring the correct heavy-duty equipment.

What items will you NOT move?

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot transport hazardous materials (gasoline, paint, chemicals), firearms/ammunition, or live pets/plants.

Service Area

What areas do you cover?

Our primary local area includes St. George, Washington, Hurricane, Santa Clara, and Ivins. We also perform long-distance moves to Las Vegas, Salt Lake City, and Phoenix.

Are there travel fees?

For local moves within Washington County, there are typically no travel fees. For locations outside our standard radius, a travel fee may apply based on mileage.

Day-of-Move

How should I prepare for the movers?

Please ensure small items are packed in boxes and taped shut. Clear walkways of tripping hazards. Secure pets in a safe room or crate. If you need us to pack for you, please arrange this in advance.

What if it rains or snows?

We move rain or shine! We use extra floor runners and coverings to protect your home and belongings. In severe weather events (blizzards/ice) that pose a safety risk, we will contact you to reschedule.

Still have questions?

We are happy to answer any other questions you might have. Give us a call!
